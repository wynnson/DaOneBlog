Revolution Won't Change Us


Human nature is not yet advanced enough for another great revolution to change the grand scheme of society. After another uproarious rebellion and streets stained red, time will inevitably topple this new regime. A revolutionary leader inevitably get replaced by the corrupt and the incompotent. The next revolution won't change us - and that is by design of our nature. Whether tested by God or created through some simulation, humanity's ultimate test will be whether we are able to escape from our own nature. It is by human nature to chase perfection, hoard possessions, and exploit obsessions. By design, we were created parasitic, almost a test of whether we can overcome our flesh. The only revolution capable of evolving society will shed no blood. It will come from a conscious realization that the test starts every day. But at that point, humanity will no longer be human.
